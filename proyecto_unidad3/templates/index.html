Cisco IOS XE is widely used in enterprise and service provider environments due to its robustness, flexibility, and the wide range of features it offers for the management and operation of modern networks.

Here are some key points about Cisco IOS XE:

Cisco IOS XE supports RESTful APIs and YANG data models, facilitating network automation and integration with network management and orchestration tools.

Cisco IOS XE is based on a modular architecture that allows different processes to run independently. This improves the system's stability and resilience, as a failure in one module does not affect the entire operating system.
